Chocolate Chip Cookies

Grandma Esters cookies are to die for! They are soft, chewy, and chocolaty.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of all-purpose
  • ½ teaspoon of baking soda
  • ½ teaspon salt
  • ¾ cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 1 egg
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees °F
  2. Mix flour, baking soda, and salt and set aside.
  3. In a separate bowl, mix melted butter, brown sugar and white sugar.
  4. Add the egg, egg yolk till theres a creamy consistency.
  5. Add the Ingredients you set aside in step 2.
  6. Add chocolate chips and blend.
  7. Add ½ cup of cookie dough in a pan separated 3 inches apart from each other.
  8. Place in the oven, and bake around 15 to 17 minutes until edges are cripsy.
  9. Let cookies cool in a wire rack.
