Chicken Noodle Soup

This soul-warming soup is deliciously simple -- just chicken and noodles. What makes it so good is the homemade roasted chicken stock.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • ½ cup diced carrot
  • ½ cup diced onion
  • ½ cup diced celery
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • ¼ teaspoon fresh thyme leaves
  • 2 tablespoons melted chicken fat
  • 2 qt roasted chicken broth
  • 4 oz uncooked wide egg noodles
  • 2 coocked chicken breasts, cubed
  • 1 pinch salt and ground black pepper

Steps:

  1. Melt butter in a large soup pot & stir in veggies, thyme, and a pinch of salt.
  2. Stir in chicken fat and still until onions turn soft and translucent.
  3. Add chicken broth and bring to a boil.
  4. Stir in egg noodles, cook until tender.
  5. Add chicken breast and simmer for about 5 minutes.
  6. Season to taste.
